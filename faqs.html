General

What is Lagibet?

Lagibet is a competitive skill based gaming app. You play games and compete with others on score, high scorers win cash prizes. All games are skill based, so get your game up!

What are championships? New

Championships are time based contests with a limited number of players. You enter any live championship by paying an entry fee. Once you enter, make sure you get a good score to rank high. Your winning amount is based on your rank.

How to enter a championship?

You enter any live championship by paying an entry fee.

I scored the same as my opponent, why did he win?

In case of the same scores we look at other variables like time taken etc.. to determine the winner.

My account is blocked. What can I do now?

'Lagibet' supports fair play and has zero tolerance for any user found to be using tricks, hacks or other unnatural behavior to play games and quizzes. We have stringent automated and manual checks that decide if an account should be blocked.

If your account has been temporarily suspended then it could be due to 2 reasons :

  • We found suspicious activity- These are violations of our 'Terms of Service' and these accounts can not be unblocked. In some cases we may also press legal charges against users committing fraud.
  • Your KYC is pending- You need to submit your KYC and it can take upto 7 days for us to verify your account. Once it is verified your account will be active again.
  • Bonus Wallet- All the cash rewards earned from referrals will come in this wallet.These can be used to play games but can not be withdrawn.

To appeal write to support@lagibet.io
