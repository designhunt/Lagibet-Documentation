Refund And Cancellation Policy

Last updated: October 8th, 2020

General

Our focus is complete user satisfaction. Please read all the rules, terms & conditions, privacy policies before entering Lagibet. Our Policy for the cancellation and refund will be as follows:

Refund Policy

We will try our best to create the best user & gaming experience for our users.

If paid by credit card, refunds will be issued to the original credit card provided at the time of payment, & in case of payment made through payment gateway, payments will be refunded in the same account.

Cancellation Policy

For Cancellations please contact us by writing us on support@lagibet.io

We do not cancel game registrations once a user has registered for a particular game by paying for it however, in case of exceptional circumstances wherein the fault may lie with the payment gateway or from our side, we will cancel your order on request and refund the money back to you within a reasonable amount of time.
