Introduction

So that you are here you must be eager to read our story. The Lagibet app is a fun pop culture quiz app and is managed and owned by Mccandless Management Private Limited.

A few months back a team of pop culture enthusiasts at Mccandless Management Private Limited came up with the idea of launching a pop culture quiz app in India. At Lagibet we are proud that we make the Baazigars out of pop culture lovers.

Lagibet is currently associated with a growing fan base and moving ahead with vigorous speed.
